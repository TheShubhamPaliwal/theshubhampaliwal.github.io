About Me

I'm a self-taught content creator, video editor, and storyteller with a passion for making technology simple and easy to understand.

I create technology explainers and documentary-style videos that break down complex topics into simple, engaging stories. From everyday gadgets and the internet to artificial intelligence and future innovations, my goal is to help people understand how technology works through clear explanations, research, and visual storytelling.

I believe learning should be interesting, not complicated. That's why I focus on turning technical concepts into stories that are easy to follow and enjoyable to watch.

My long-term vision is to create an impact beyond the digital world. Along with making educational technology content, I hope to contribute to meaningful initiatives that support sustainable future.
